About ShelemJ

Rooted in Africa.
Trusted by Retailers Worldwide.

Founded in 2013 as Jueco Foods, ShelemJ began its journey by specializing in the export of African-made food and raw materials to European markets, including the Netherlands and Belgium. In 2021, the company rebranded to Emma Worldwide, before undergoing a strategic restructuring aimed at enhancing our service and outreach to clients, ultimately emerging as ShelemJ.

With over a decade of expertise in the export of premium African food, we are dedicated to providing quality, consistency, and cultural richness to retailers and grocery stores in international markets.
